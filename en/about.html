Company Profile

Powernet techonology Ltd. is founded in 2017. The team has a great mix of passion, experience and is led by people who have shipped multiple products globally. We enhance product and market values to our clients in the form of enthralling, fascinating, and addictive games. With the help of our professional and experienced team that comprises of Technical Consultants, UI designers, Code developers, Team Leads and Web Designers, we aim to spell success for you when it comes to the field of games development!

Business Concepts

In advantage of our expertise in internet and network securities, Powernet provides our customers with unique and customized solutions. We are located in Taichung but work on a global vision to build our and your future.

Global Vision

In the world of online games, a simple network connection would serve you any visual and audio entertainments at any time and places. That is what Powernet do, we serve our clients globally with customized services and products.

Flat Organization

We believe that a flat organization would avoid inefficient process and management. A free and efficient working culture help us to be closer to market trends which also give our team more flexibility in their talent development.

Technology and Innovation

As technology and data analysis advance, big data is frequently used, more specifically for managing data from different sources. We managed, analysed and optimized these data, and integrated the results into a system. In order to enhace its capacity, Socket, Serverlet, HTTP, Message Queue are applied; we also use MongoDB which is classified as a huge NoSQL database, which can agilely create an index for JSON, and is also highly efficient and expandable. A great system integration helps us to reduce extra work and makes the process simpler and more cost-efficient. These are key success factors of Powernet Technology.
